Welcome

CreeperCraft is a little survival server on the Bedrock version of Minecraft, to join you need to put in creepercraft.online as the IP address and know the secret password off of one of us :)

Mods

The server will probably be lightly modded with some basic stuff to make the game more fun. Probably something like:

  • Player head drops sometimes when someone kills you
  • Maybe some kind of pipeline thing so we can make factories
  • Trees falling over?

Nothing too modded, it'll be basically vanilla!

Hardware

Right now it will be ran off of a DigitalOcean droplet with about 4GB of RAM, a decent CPU, and some storage. This will be enough to support like 5 players with no issues at all, 10 players will probs the the happy limit. In the future I'll buy a mini-pc which will be way better than a Raspberry Pi so we should be good for power.
