Welcome

CreeperCraft is a little survival server on the Bedrock version of Minecraft, to join you need to put in creepercraft.online as the IP address and be whitelisted by one of us :)

Mods

Due to limitations in the Bedrock edition of Minecraft, this server will be vanilla. It would be nice to have a few minor plugins installed on the server but it will be fully vanilla for the time being!

Hardware

Right now it will be ran off of a DigitalOcean droplet with about 4GB of RAM, a decent CPU, and some storage. This will be enough to support like 5 players with no issues at all, 10 players will probs the the happy limit. In the future I'll buy a mini-pc which will be way better than a Raspberry Pi so we should be good for power.
